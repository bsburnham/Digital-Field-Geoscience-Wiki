## How to Add Yourself

If you've contributed to this wiki, you're welcome to add yourself to the Contributors section above by including your addition in your pull request.

**Format:**

```
* **Your Name** - Your Affiliation (optional) - Brief contribution description (optional)
```

**Examples:**

```
* **Jane Smith** - University of Example - Added section on UAV photogrammetry workflows
* **John Doe** - Fixed formatting errors in equipment lists
* **Alex Johnson** - British Geological Survey - Contributed case study on digital stratigraphic logging
```
